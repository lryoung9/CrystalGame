Crystal Collector

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specified amount of points to your score.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it and you see the increase in your score.
  • You win the game by matching your score to the goal. You will lose if your total score exceeds the goal.
  • Each time the game starts again, the game will change the value of the crystals.
